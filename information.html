About Us:




Summer at westgress is a programme ran by westgress council, who recently took it over from Westgress Academy Of Sports And Science, is a sports programme ran within the summer holiday's. Not only does it aim to get kids fitter, but anyone who is in their GCSE Year(s) we offer free tutoring after all the sports are completed.
